Part 3: Evaluation

a): Self Evaluation

First off, I want to start off how I conquered my fear of height on this trip, I was completely against rock climbing the minute I saw the height. I did not want to go up, but since we had incredible instructors, I got over my fear and completed the activity, and now I just want to go back to rock climb. Overall, I did't cause trouble, I led the canoe ride back, and helped set up tarps, cook with Peter, and set up a fire. Behavioural wise, I think I was good because I didn't cause mischief or cause trouble, I took the initiative with Peter to try to make a bridge over a small path of water, so we didn't have to get our feet wet.

b): Class Evaluation

Group Evaluation: 9.5/10 I think everyone had a really good time at Strathcona, learning new things, like rock climbing, and facing your fears while doing so. I think people really enjoyed the adventure to their islands as well, even though rowing can be hard, the effort led to an amazing overnight stay that left everyone with an unforgettable time. We didn't cause any trouble while we were there, so I think we did a really good job showing that we are a nice school. Everyone was in their rooms on time and asleep on time, everyone followed the rules we were given, so that is incredible.
