English 10

For English 10 were assigned 2 books and a play.
The first book we read was To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee.
This book, in a way teaches the reader lessons about equality and empathy.
After that, we read Othello by William Shakespeare.
It sent a similar message as To Kill a Mockingbird about not making judgements of people without actual reason, and also about racism at that time period.
Lastly, we read and studied Divergent by Veronica Roth.
This was a little bit different than the play and the book before that we read.
The book was more modern, but still gave a look at separation among society and different factions instead of races.
